Movich Casa del Alférez

Cali, Colombia
Overview Images Rooms Reviews Location

A blend of period style and cosmopolitan chic in Cali, where old world meets new

On a peaceful street, the grand façade of Movich Casa del Alférez opens up into a light and airy lobby. Dark wood contrasts with white walls and polished, marble floors, dotted with pieces of modern art. And the intricate iron balustrades lend the interior a historic charm.

Close to the sights and sounds of the lively centre of Cali, this luxury hotel offers travellers a hip haven. Sip pre-dinner drinks in the outdoor terrace bar, lit by lanterns and candles. Enjoy fine dining in La Maria restaurant. Or simply lounge on your private balcony and watch the world go by.

When you’re ready to go outside and explore, the sights and nightlife of Cali are minutes from the hotel. Discover the area’s historical roots at the 16th-century Iglesia de la Merced or the neo-Gothic Iglesia de la Ermita. Take a short taxi ride to the popular Chipichape shopping mall – a former warehouse and train station – to indulge in a spot of retail therapy. Or give in to the infectious Latin rhythms and try out your moves at one of the city’s salsa bars.


Enjoy your stay!

Food And Drinks

La Maria restaurant serves up traditional South American cuisine and international gastronomy. The white linen tablecloths, chic furniture, gentle lighting and exposed brick walls give the restaurant a cool, calm look and feel. After dinner, head to the stylish bar that spills out onto a terrace – an idyllic spot for a sundowner.

Iraka Bar

On our terrace you will find the Iraka Bar. This is a magical place cooled by the Juanambu breeze. It is the place to be on Thursday and Friday nights for the best live music. The bar serves delicious cocktails and other alcoholic drinks for you to enjoy whilst you select some exquisite snacks from our menu. Do not miss our delicious bruschettas topped with red tuna or fresh prawns from the pacific. Enjoy the music and the breeze with a drink and the exquisite seafood from the Colombian sea.

La Maria

A menu created with original ingredients and signature recipes. A menu that proposes seven different types of food and allows an explosion of flavours and you can continue a good night with the best live music in the Iraka bar. In this environment you will enjoy delicious drinks that can be complemented with delicious tapas or with one of our themed nights of Wok and skewers. Sunday lunches are full of local flavour as are family breakfasts on the terrace or afternoon tea and cakes.

Spa

To burn off some energy, step inside the Movich Casa del Alférez gym. Or, after a day exploring Cali’s landmarks and boutiques, sink into the spa tub and revive tired limbs.
